Here is the list of projects I've done...

XLR8

A bot controlled by bluetooth using your handset, custom made to pass an obastacle course. It is powered by a lead-acid battery and the motors are controlled by a microcontroller. I worked with Amit and Hitesh to build it.

Invisibility Cloak

Sounds cool, doesn't it? I used Python and OpenCV library to mimic Harry Potter's Invisibility cloak. It captures the color of the cloak and replaces that part of the picture with the background.

Sudoku Solver

Another computer vision project which detects a sudoku board from live feed , uses machine learning to recognise digits in their respective slots and solves the puzzle using algorithms.

Virtual Keyboard

Did you know that you can give input to your computer using just a printed keyboard layout, a camera and a program? The camera feeds in live video to the computer and the frames are processed using OpenCV(python) to detect the key tapped by your finger and taking the input correspondingly.

Water Reminder app

A GUI application to remind you to drink water at regular intervals. Akash and I made it using Python and Kivy Framework.
